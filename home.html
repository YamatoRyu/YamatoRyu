Aqours

Aqours is a school idol group that was originally formed and led by Matsuura Kanan (松浦果南) two years prior to falling apart and was eventually succeeded by Takami Chika (高海千歌) in Love Live! Sunshine!!.
They were the school idol group of Uranohoshi Girls' High School. The original group consisted of three members, while the new group consisted of nine members.
At the beginning of the movie, the group continues with six of the members at Numazu High School following their temporary integration at Seishin High School. They debuted with the song Kimi no Kokoro wa Kagayaiteru kai? or in japanese kanji is 君のこころは輝いてるかい？.

Season 1

Chika Takami, a self-proclaimed normal girl, has never been involved in any clubs and lacked any notable talents.However, after a visit to Tokyo, she discovers a stage where even an ordinary girl like her could shine—the world of school idols, inspired by the former superstar school idol group μ's.

Season 2

Having decided to walk down a path separate from their inspirations as idols, the girls of Aqours are more determined than ever to shine brilliantly as school idols. With their previous performance marginally increasing the number of prospective students for their endangered school.

Over The Rainbow

Following the closure of Uranohoshi Girls' High School, the third-year students Dia Kurosawa, Kanan Matsuura, and Mari Ohara have just graduated, leaving Aqours with solely the first and second-years. While searching for a new place the remaining members can use to practice.
